Our Services

Men's Embroidery

At Kashaf Enterprises, we bring timeless artistry to men's fashion through embroidery that blends cultural heritage with modern refinement. From subtle elegance to bold statements, each stitch reflects precision and purpose.

Chikankari

Chikankari is a delicate and intricate hand-embroidery technique that brings grace and refinement to men's kurtas. Its fine white threadwork on light fabrics speaks of timeless craftsmanship and understated beauty.

Phulkari

Phulkari, or “flower work,” embodies vibrant colors and expressive patterns. It transforms men’s shawls and jackets into statements of tradition and culture.

Zardozi

Zardozi embroidery adds royal charm through metallic threads and embellishments, perfect for sherwanis and ceremonial wear that exude opulence.

Shaneel

The key feature of Shaneel (or chenille) embroidery is its soft, carpet-like pile, which is made from yarn rather than flat embroidery thread. This creates a three-dimensional effect and a rich feel.

Appliqué

Appliqué embroidery layers fabrics with precision to create striking visual depth — a blend of creativity and structure.





Women's Embroidery

Women’s embroidery celebrates elegance, color, and expression — from timeless handwork to intricate thread artistry. Each stitch narrates a story of creativity, culture, and feminine grace.

Aari

Aari embroidery showcases fine chain stitches worked using a hooked needle, creating stunning floral and paisley patterns that add luxury to sarees, blouses, and dupattas.

Chikankari

Known for its delicate white threadwork, Chikankari adds soft charm and grace to women’s wear — symbolizing purity and sophistication across generations.

Zardozi

Regal and ornate, Zardozi embroidery features metallic threads, beads, and sequins — bringing grandeur to bridal and festive ensembles.

Cross-Stitch

Cross-stitch embroidery features intersecting X-shaped stitches, offering structured yet vibrant designs ideal for decorative linens and garments.

Whitework

Elegant and subtle, Whitework uses white threads on white fabrics, creating graceful, tone-on-tone patterns — a true expression of minimal beauty.





Digitizing, Designing & Cut-Packing

Beyond embroidery, our expertise extends into the essential processes that make every garment production-ready — blending technology, artistry, and precision.

Digitizing

Our team converts intricate designs into precise digital embroidery files using advanced software, ensuring every stitch follows the designer’s intent — where technology meets craftsmanship.

Designing

From conceptual sketches to full-scale embroidery layouts, our creative designers bring ideas to life — crafting visuals that inspire, impress, and tell your brand’s story.

Cut-Packing

After the embroidery is perfected, our expert team carefully trims, checks, and packs every piece — ready for dispatch with the same attention to detail that defines Kashaf Enterprises.
